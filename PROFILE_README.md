### 🔭 Research interests
Medical AI · Laryngoscopy image analysis · Vocal fold diseases · Head-and-neck CT imaging · Segmentation · Radiomics · Explainable AI

### 🚀 Featured projects
- **AI-assisted Vocal Fold Disease Analysis** — classification, detection & segmentation of vocal fold lesions from flexible laryngoscopy
- **Head and Neck CT Lesion Segmentation** — segmentation-guided analysis of abscess, tumor, and cyst lesions
- **Content-Based Medical Image Retrieval** — retrieval via segmentation, radiomics, and deep imaging features
- **Mobile AI for ENT Screening** — lightweight models for near-real-time ENT endoscopy support
